Tree Of Life.

Saving Children with Quality Health Care.

We join hands to ensure better lives for underprivileged children through comprehensive medical care

DQF joins hands with Aster DM Healthcare and KITES India to launch Tree of Life, a noble initiative for children from underprivileged sections of our society. The project will assure quality healthcare, accessible to children by providing life-saving surgeries and necessary interventional procedures free of cost through Aster Hospitals across Kerala. It is deemed to be launched on November 14th, on the eve of Children’s Day.

Our Vision

  • To save lives of underprivileged children, securing their access to quality medical care.
  • To help their families from the vicious cycle of poverty that could arise from mounting medical costs.
  • To support children from underprivileged sections to thrive against the odds and be an asset to their families and our nation.

WHAT?

DQF joins hands with Aster DM Healthcare and KITES India to launch Tree of Life, a noble initiative for children from underprivileged sections of our society. The project will assure quality healthcare, accessible to children by providing life-saving surgeries and necessary interventional procedures free of cost through Aster Hospitals across Kerala. It is deemed to be launched on November 14th, on the eve of Children’s Day.

WHY?

Children are undoubtedly the future of tomorrow. It is painful to see thousands of children perishing due to unaffordable and inaccessible medical care. Medical costs of diseased children, often push economically poor families into even deeper crises. What if we join hands to save the lives of hundreds of children from underprivileged families offering them advanced, quality medical care and pediatric surgeries free of cost?
Tree Of Life in association with Aster DM Healthcare and KITES India ensures life-saving surgeries and necessary interventional procedures free of cost, through Aster Hospitals across Kerala.

How?

Tree of Life caters to Pediatrics Surgeries exclusively for underprivileged children. The initiative secures the beneficiaries advanced treatments and post-operative care. The entire treatment procedure will be done under the supervision of experienced eminent Department Leads at Aster Hospitals across Kerala. (Aster Medcity - Kochi, Aster MIMS - Calicut, Aster MIMS - Kottakkal, Aster MIMS - Kannur, and Aster Mother Hospital Areekode.)

The initiative will also meet the additional medical expenses for children who cannot afford it. Financial assistance will be provided to the children through Aster DM Foundation & other Crowdfunding platforms.

Surgeries include the following departments.

  • Paediatric Cardio, Gastro, Neurology, Oncology, Ortho, Urology
  • Paediatric Liver, Kidney Transplant Surgeries
  • Paediatric Bone Marrow Transplant
  • Liver & Kidney Transplant
  • Bone Marrow & Stem Cell Transplant
  • Cardiology & Cardiac Surgery

KITES INDIA

They have an impressive growth rate of volunteers across the nation and a presence in 12 states.
